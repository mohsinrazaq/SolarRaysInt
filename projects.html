Safety First

Flights are possible at any time of the year, but only under safe weather conditions. No flights allowed when it's raining heavily. We carefully check the wind speed. If there is something wrong with the weather, we'll refund you the full cost of tickets. We can set another flight time if you're interested.

More Fun

Rent a full basket for an hour. Only our instructor will be with you in the basket. Also, we can offer some nice supplements. If you want to make your date special, we can offer to you some champagne or flowers. Check our prices!

Friends are Welcomed

We offer a big discount if you are a group of four people. If you're a family, you can also use this discount. Please note that our baskets are limited: four is the maximal amount of people in the basket during one flight. Our experienced instructor will be in the basket.
